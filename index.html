Who We Are

We are Paws & Purrs Photography, a dedicated pet photography studio with clients across the globe. We capture the unique charm of pets through stunning photoshoots and beautiful visual storytelling.

We offer a range of services including pet portfolios, themed photoshoots, and action shots. Our skilled photographers ensure a comfortable experience for your pets. Whether you're looking for a holiday special, a pet and owner session, or a custom shoot, we create lasting memories with professional care and creativity. Join our community of pet lovers and cherish the moments with your furry friends through our lens.

What we do

We take pride in what we do. Our services are designed to help your business stand out and turn your ideas into digital realities.

Pet Portfolios

We create comprehensive photo sessions that capture the unique personalities and expressions of your pets. Our skilled photographers ensure every shot is perfect, delivering a collection of stunning images you’ll treasure forever. Our portfolios highlight the beauty and charm of your beloved pets, preserving precious memories.

Themed photoshoots

Our themed photoshoots offer seasonal and holiday-themed sessions. From festive Christmas shoots to spooky Halloween setups, we create a fun and engaging environment for your pets. These special themes provide a creative way to celebrate occasions and capture your pets in delightful, themed settings.

Custom Sessions

We offer tailored photoshoots based on your unique requests, including birthday parties, special events, and more. Our custom sessions are designed to meet your specific needs, providing a personalized experience that captures the essence of your pet's special moments. We work closely with you to create the perfect setting and atmosphere for your custom shoot.

Pet and Owner Sessions

Our pet and owner sessions are designed to capture the bond between you and your furry friends. These sessions create heartwarming photos that celebrate your relationship. Our photographers work to ensure both you and your pets are comfortable, resulting in genuine, candid moments that reflect your connection.

Newborn Pet Sessions

Celebrate the arrival of a new pet with our newborn pet sessions. These special photoshoots focus on the early days of your pet's life, capturing their innocence and adorableness. We create a relaxed and safe environment for your new pet, ensuring beautiful and memorable images.

Senior Pet Portraits

Our senior pet portraits honor the lives of older pets with dignified and heartfelt photoshoots. We focus on capturing the wisdom and grace of your senior pets, creating lasting tributes to their long and cherished lives. These sessions are a beautiful way to celebrate the journey and companionship of your beloved pets.
